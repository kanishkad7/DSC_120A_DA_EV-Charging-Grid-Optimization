Introduction
The rapid rise of electric vehicles (EVs) is reshaping energy consumption patterns and placing new demands on electrical grids. Understanding how, when, and where EV charging occurs is essential for ensuring grid stability and planning future infrastructure. This project analyzes EV charging and grid optimization data to uncover behavioral patterns, identify stress points, and support data‑driven decision‑making for sustainable grid management.

Problem Statement
The increasing adoption of electric vehicles is introducing unpredictable and concentrated loads on electrical grids. Utilities currently lack clear insights into EV charging behavior and its impact on grid performance. This uncertainty makes it challenging to plan infrastructure upgrades, manage peak loads, and maintain reliable grid operations.
